Nature Credits are a digital product designed to attract Investment to financially support nature projects.

Think carbon credits, but for nature, with less drama and more accessible to anyone who wants to support the natural environment, or to help mitigate potential reputational or financial risk.

Nature Credits can be used to promote or build your brand by attracting a more environmentally conscious consumer that demands purpose attached to purchase.

The data used for Nature Credits is also used to support, monitor or create new policy's and legislation to further ensure the success and longevity of the nature project.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Measurable Nature Project

Project Operator: Seabin Location: Sydney Australia Biome: Ocean Water Quality, Microplastics, Plastic Pollution, Marine Litter

Science Based Methodology

Water filtered over time. Sub sampling of microplastics, plastic pollution, and marine litter

Fractionalised Pricing & Credit Scheme

Cost of project fractionalised into smaller amounts. Packaged, managed and sold as credits and certificates

Reporting, Communications, and Promotion

Report on your nature positive impact for ESG, sustainability or shareholder purposes. Promote your impact for marketing purposes
